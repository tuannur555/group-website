HOW TO CONNECT TO NETWORK?



HOW TO CONNECT TO WIFI





1

Click the network icon at status bar.














2

Click on Wi-Fi to connect to the network














3

Click on any network that you want to connect









4

Click connect.










5

Insert password and click next.








6

You are connected.






HOW TO CONNECT BLUETOOTH






1

Click Bluetooth device at status bar.










2

Select 'Add a Bluetooth device'.









3

Turn on the Bluetooth device and click ‘Add Bluetooth or other devices’.











4

Select a bluetooth device.











5

Make sure your device is turn on and select the device to connect.











6

Finally, the Bluetooth are connected.
