ADDITIONAL FUNCTION TO ADD TO COMPUTER



The purpose of these devices is to translate the machine's response to a usable form for the computer user. Input devices are the hardware that give computers instructions. Output devices relay the response from the computer in the form of a visual response (monitor), sound (speakers) or media devices. If you have a printer, webcam, speaker or other peripherals, you can connect them at this point. Other peripherals may include software that needs to be installed before you can begin using them. Use the instructions included with the device to install it if necessary.





1. PRINTER

The most common way to connect a printer to your PC is by USB cable, which makes it a local printer. You can also install a wireless printer or add a printer connected to another laptop or PC on your network. We'll cover these scenarios below:








Step 1: Connect the printer to your computer using the USB cable and turn it on.



Step 2: Open the Settings app from the Start menu.



Step 3: Click Devices.



Step 4: Click Add a printer or scanner



Step 5: If Windows detects your printer, click on the name of the printer and follow the on-screen instructions to finish the installation. And you're done. If Windows doesn't find your connected printer, click on "the printer that I wanted isn't listed".





  • Then let the Windows troubleshooting guide help you find your printer. It will search for available printers and help you download the drivers for them.

  • If that doesn't work, head to your printer manufacturer's website and download the drivers and installation tools for your printer.













    • 2. WEBCAM








    Step 1: Open the Settings app by pressing the Windows + I shortcut key, or clicking the Settings icon from the Start Menu in Windows 10.





    Step 2: From the Settings window, click Privacy.





    Step 3: Choose Camera in the left pane. You’ll see an option that says “Let apps use my camera”. Slide the toggle to On and select which of the apps underneath can use your camera, or toggle Off to prevent all apps from accessing your camera.











    3. WIRELESS SPEAKER/ BLUETOOTH DEVICE

    This is one of the example wireless speaker or Bluetooth device that you can use and connect to your computer. Wireless speakers have been widely used to provide music throughout a house by the touch of a button.








    Step 1: Press your speaker's "Power" button to turn it on.

  • You need to make sure the “power” button has light after you press it, this is help you to know that your speaker is in a good condition.






  • You are also need to make sure that your speaker is as close to your computer as possible while pairing. The distance between speaker and your computer will effect of the connection to the speaker.










    • Step 2: Open your Bluetooth in your computer.

  • Open your computer and go to the windows settings. Click “devices”










  • After that, click the on Bluetooth to turn on your speaker. The speaker enters pairing mode automatically by just turning on the speaker.












  • Choose your devices and click. It will be connected after you click on your devices.




  • If the connection is not established, repeat from the first step.




  • If you want to disconnected your device with your computer, just turn off the Bluetooth in your computer and you can also press the “power” button on your speaker to shut down your speaker.
